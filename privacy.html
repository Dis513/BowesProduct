Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how BowesProduct ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website or purchase our 3D assets. We are committed to transparency and only handle the minimum data required to provide our services. Last updated: December 27, 2025.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to process purchases and improve our service:

  • Purchase information: When you buy an asset via Stripe (our payment processor), we receive your email address, transaction ID, and purchased items. We do not store or have access to your payment details (credit card, etc.).
  • Website analytics: We use basic analytics (via Plausible or similar privacy-friendly tools) to understand site traffic and usage patterns. No personal identifiers or cookies are used for tracking individuals.
  • No account registration: You do not need to create an account. Purchases are linked to the email provided during checkout.

2. How We Use Your Information

The limited data we collect is used solely for operational purposes:

  • To deliver purchased assets (instant download links are sent via email).
  • To send transactional emails (purchase confirmation, download links).
  • To improve our website and product offerings based on anonymous usage data.
  • To comply with legal obligations if required.

We never use your information for marketing, advertising, or any unrelated purposes.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

To further protect your privacy, we explicitly avoid collecting unnecessary data:

  • No cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.
  • No personal data beyond what is required for purchase fulfillment.
  • No sale or sharing of your data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • No behavioral profiling or targeted advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party providers for essential functions:

  • Stripe: Payments are processed securely by Stripe. Their privacy policy applies to data handled during payment: https://stripe.com/privacy
  • Hosting: The website is hosted on a static hosting provider (e.g., Vercel/Netlify). They may log standard server access data but do not share personal information with us.

5. Data Security

We take data protection seriously and implement reasonable security measures, including encryption for transactions and secure storage practices, to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have control over your personal data. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information at any time by contacting us. We will respond promptly and free of charge within a reasonable timeframe, typically 30 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any modifications will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out through our contact channels. We're here to help and will respond as quickly as possible.
