Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how it’s used, and how we protect it when you visit BowesProduct or make a purchase.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Process and complete purchases
  • Respond to support inquiries

Third-Party Services

Payments are processed by Stripe. We never store your credit card details. Stripe’s own privacy and security practices apply.

Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information, but no system is 100% secure. If you ever have concerns, contact us at hello@bowesproduct.com.

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies or tracking tools for analytics and performance monitoring, but we do not sell or share personal information with advertisers.
