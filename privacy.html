Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how BowesProduct ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website or purchase our 3D assets.

1. Information We Collect

  • Purchase information: When you buy an asset via Stripe, we receive your email address, transaction ID, and purchased items. We do not store payment details.
  • Website analytics: We use privacy-friendly analytics to understand general site usage. No personal identifiers are tracked.
  • No required accounts: Purchases are linked to the email provided at checkout.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver purchased assets and download links
  • Send transactional emails
  • Improve website performance and offerings
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No advertising or tracking cookies
  • No sale of personal data
  • No unnecessary personal information

4. Third-Party Services

Stripe: Payments are processed securely by Stripe. Their privacy policy applies: https://stripe.com/privacy

Hosting: Hosted on a static platform which may log standard access data.

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable security practices, but no method of online transmission is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

8. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our website.
