Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines the types of personal information we collect and how we use, protect, and share it.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you create an account, make a purchase, or contact us for support. This may include your name, email address, billing information, and any other relevant data necessary to process transactions and support requests.

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used to process payments, deliver purchased products, and improve our services. We may also use your information to contact you with updates or promotional offers related to our products, with an option to opt-out.

Data Security

We take security seriously and use encryption to protect sensitive data during transactions. We do not store payment details on our servers. Any information shared is stored securely and only accessible to authorized personnel.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@bowesproduct.com.

Third-Party Services

We may share your information with third-party services, such as payment processors or email service providers, to assist in fulfilling your order or communicating with you. These services are obligated to use your information solely for those purposes.
