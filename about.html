About BowesProduct

BowesProduct is an independent 3D asset studio focused on creating high-quality, production-ready 3D models for developers, artists, and studios.

All assets are designed with clean topology, optimized performance, and compatibility across modern pipelines including Blender, Unity, Unreal Engine, and Godot.
