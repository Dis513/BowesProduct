About BowesProduct

Creating professional, production-ready 3D character models and props for indie developers, studios, and creators worldwide.

Who We Are

BowesProduct is a one-person independent 3D asset studio focused on delivering high-quality, commercially licensed 3D models that are ready to drop into your game, animation, or VR/AR project.

Every model is carefully crafted with clean topology, optimized performance, PBR materials, and full rigging — built from years of experience in game development and real-time pipelines.

Our Philosophy

We believe great tools should be accessible. That's why we offer:

  • Fair, one-time pricing with no royalties
  • Unlimited commercial use license
  • Instant downloads in .glb and .fbx formats
  • Engine-ready assets for Unity, Unreal, Godot, Blender, and more
  • Regular updates and new releases
