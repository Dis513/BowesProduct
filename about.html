About BowesProduct

Who We Are

BowesProduct is a one-person independent 3D asset studio focused on delivering high-quality, commercially licensed 3D models ready for real production.

Every model is carefully crafted with clean topology, optimized performance, PBR materials, and full rigging — built from years of experience in real-time pipelines.

Our Philosophy

  • Fair one-time pricing, no royalties
  • Unlimited commercial use license
  • Instant downloads (.glb & .fbx)
  • Engine-ready for Unity, Unreal, Godot, Blender
  • Regular updates and new releases
