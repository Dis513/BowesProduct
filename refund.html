Refund Policy

Clear and simple rules for refunds on BowesProduct assets

Eligibility

Refunds are only provided in exceptional cases such as duplicate charges, accidental purchases, or technical issues preventing delivery. Digital products cannot be returned once delivered.

How to Request a Refund

  • Contact support@bowesproduct.com within 7 days of purchase
  • Provide purchase receipt and description of issue
  • Refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis

Processing Refunds

Approved refunds are processed via the original payment method. It may take 5–10 business days for the refund to reflect.

Non-Refundable Cases

  • Change of mind after download
  • Partial usage of digital assets
  • Violation of License or Terms
