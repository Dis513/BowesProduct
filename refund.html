Refund Policy

How refunds are handled for BowesProduct digital assets

Eligibility

Due to the nature of digital products, refunds are only granted in exceptional circumstances, such as duplicate charges, accidental purchases, or technical issues preventing access.

Requesting a Refund

  • Contact support@bowesproduct.com within 7 days of purchase
  • Include proof of purchase and a description of the issue
  • All requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processing times typically range from 5–10 business days.

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after download or access
  • Partial or full use of digital assets
  • Violations of the License or Terms of Service
