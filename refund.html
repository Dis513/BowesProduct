Refund Policy

This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued for purchases made on BowesProduct.

1. Digital Products

All products sold on BowesProduct are digital downloads. Once a product has been delivered or made available for download, it is considered used.

2. No Automatic Refunds

Due to the nature of digital goods, all sales are final and non-refundable.

3. Eligible Refund Exceptions

  • The file is corrupted or unusable and a replacement cannot be provided
  • The product was purchased in error and has not been downloaded
  • Duplicate purchases of the same product

4. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after download
  • Lack of software compatibility
  • Failure to read product descriptions or license terms
  • Violation of the License Agreement

5. Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please contact BowesProduct support with your order details and reason for the request. Requests must be made within a reasonable time after purchase.
