Refund Policy

How refunds are handled for BowesProduct digital assets

BowesProduct Refund Policy

Due to the instant-download nature of digital products, refunds are granted only in limited circumstances.

Eligibility for Refund

Refunds may be issued in the following cases:

  • Accidental duplicate purchase
  • Technical error preventing download or access to files
  • Charged incorrectly or without authorization

How to Request a Refund

  • Contact us at support@bowesproduct.com within 7 days of purchase
  • Include your transaction ID, email used for purchase, and a clear description of the issue
  • We review all requests individually and respond within 2–3 business days

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method via Stripe. Processing typically takes 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after successful download or access
  • Partial or full use of the purchased assets
  • Requests made more than 7 days after purchase
  • Violation of our License Agreement or Terms of Service

We aim to ensure a fair and transparent process. Thank you for understanding the limitations of digital product sales.
